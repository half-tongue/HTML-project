A Little About Me

I was born in a city called Maracaibo, located in the northwestern region of Venezuela. I stayed there for about a year, after  which I moved here, to Calgary, and have been here ever since! I currently am living at home with my loving parents and my younger  sister. Most of my days are spent at SAIT, as I am currently in my first semester of the Interactive Design program at SAIT, although  I foresee choosing a different path for my future. On my free time, you are most likely to catch me slamming back some yummy food,  watching movies, or cranking some tunes!!!
